64 Magic SnowFlakes

These are 64 magical multi-colored snowflakes falling from a dark moving cloud and lying on the ground. As in the old tale.

This asset is the 9th snow generator from my asset "Magic Winter VFX" 

Unity Asset Store URL:

For use:
Just add prefabs to your project and adjust their sizes. It is also possible to add a prefab with snow to the character.
