Cartoon City Pack
Asset pack to create cartoon cities.

Includes:

    More than 20 models of city buildings and props
    Modular pavement and road
    Diffuse and emission textures
    Models
    Standard materials
    Mobile materials
    Prefabs
    Simple example scene

Source files include:

    Blender files
    Textures
    UV maps

How to use:
    Just place prefabs into scene.
    If you want to change the image displayed on the billboards, just replace the texture in materials Billboard Picture and Billboard Picture 2.
